Founders:


A thought of the then ’Pranta Pracharak’ Late Somapalli Somaiahji, ‘Vibahag Prachark’, Late Sangameswara Sastri Ji and motivation of few swayam sevak’s of Nellore,Gudur and Kovur led to the foundation of Jayabharath Hosptital in 1982 on the day of Vijayadasdami, through the formation “Smhapuri Vidya Seva Samiti”.
